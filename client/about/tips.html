Tips & Instructions

New to QB Reader? Start here for quick guidance on playing, searching, and getting the most out of the site.

Getting Started
  • Use Tossups or Bonuses for singleplayer practice.
  • Join Multiplayer to play with friends or open rooms.
  • Check Settings to adjust site-wide preferences and theme.
Searching the Database
  • Open Search to find questions by text, set, or category.
  • Use Set List to browse available packets.
  • Explore Frequency List for answerline trends.
Accounts & Progress
  • Create an account to track stats and play Geoword.
  • Verify your email to unlock full account features.
  • Visit Account to manage your profile and password.
Help & Community
  • Read more on the About page.
  • Join the official Discord for support and updates.
  • Report issues or request features on GitHub.
Multiplayer
  • Join Multiplayer rooms or create your own using the search bar at the top.
  • Rooms you create are public by default; you can make them private in the sidebar.
  • Customize question settings (categories, difficulties, sets) before starting.
  • Share the room link with friends to invite them directly.
  • Use the chat feature to communicate with other players in the room.
  • Choose between tossup-only, bonus-only, or combined game modes.
